Short Description

"The Beginning After the End" follows the story of King Grey, a powerful ruler reincarnated into a magical world as Arthur Leywin. Retaining the wisdom of his past life, Arthur quickly becomes skilled in magic and combat. As he grows, he navigates a journey of self-discovery, friendship, love, and the challenge of correcting the mistakes of his former life.
